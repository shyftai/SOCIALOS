# Tool Pricing Reference — SOCIAL:OS

How each supported tool charges. Use this when setting up COSTS.md during onboarding.
Prices are approximate and change -- always verify with the tool's current pricing page.

Last updated: 2026-03

## Social Media Scheduling & Management

### Buffer
- **Model:** Per-channel subscription
- **Free tier:** 3 channels, 10 scheduled posts per channel
- **Paid plans:** Essentials $6/mo/channel, Team $12/mo/channel, Agency $120/mo (10 channels)
- **What's included:**
  - Post scheduling and queue management
  - Analytics (paid plans)
  - Engagement tools (Team+)
  - AI assistant for captions
- **API access:** All plans
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** channel/month
- **Typical cost:** $6-12 per channel per month

### Hootsuite
- **Model:** Per-seat subscription
- **Paid plans:** Professional $99/mo (1 user, 10 channels), Team $249/mo (3 users, 20 channels), Enterprise custom
- **What's included:**
  - Scheduling, monitoring, analytics
  - Social inbox
  - Content calendar
  - Team collaboration
- **API access:** Team plan and above
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** seat/month
- **Typical cost:** $99-249/mo depending on team size

### Later
- **Model:** Per-social-set subscription
- **Free tier:** 1 social set, 5 posts per profile
- **Paid plans:** Starter $25/mo (1 social set), Growth $45/mo (3 social sets), Advanced $80/mo (6 social sets)
- **What's included:**
  - Visual content calendar
  - Instagram-first features (link in bio, visual planner)
  - Analytics
  - User-generated content tools
- **API access:** Growth plan and above
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** social set/month
- **Typical cost:** $25-80/mo

### Typefully
- **Model:** Per-user subscription
- **Free tier:** Basic features, limited scheduling
- **Paid plans:** Creator $12.50/mo, Team $29/mo/user
- **What's included:**
  - Twitter/X and LinkedIn scheduling
  - Thread composer
  - Analytics and growth tools
  - AI writing assistant
  - Auto-DMs, auto-plugs (paid)
- **API access:** Creator plan and above
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** user/month
- **Typical cost:** $12.50-29/mo per user

## Newsletter Platforms

### Beehiiv
- **Model:** Subscriber-based tiers
- **Free tier:** Up to 2,500 subscribers
- **Paid plans:** Scale $49/mo (100K subs), Max $99/mo (unlimited), Enterprise custom
- **What's included:**
  - Email editor, templates
  - Built-in referral system
  - Recommendation network
  - Website/blog hosting
  - Analytics
  - Ad network (monetization)
- **API access:** All plans (Scale+ for advanced)
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** subscribers
- **Typical cost:** $0 (under 2.5K subs) to $99/mo (unlimited)

### ConvertKit (Kit)
- **Model:** Subscriber-based tiers
- **Free tier:** Up to 10,000 subscribers (limited features)
- **Paid plans:** Creator $29/mo (up to 1K subs, scales with list), Creator Pro $59/mo (up to 1K subs, scales)
- **What's included:**
  - Email sequences and automations
  - Landing pages and forms
  - Tag-based subscriber management
  - Visual automation builder (Pro)
  - Newsletter referral system (Pro)
- **API access:** All paid plans
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** subscribers
- **Typical cost:** $29-hundreds/mo depending on list size

### Substack
- **Model:** Revenue share on paid newsletters
- **Free tier:** Free forever for free newsletters
- **Paid newsletters:** Substack takes 10% of subscription revenue
- **What's included:**
  - Email publishing
  - Built-in website/blog
  - Podcast hosting
  - Community/chat features
  - Recommendation network
  - Payment processing (Stripe)
- **API access:** Limited (no official public API)
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** n/a (free for free newsletters, 10% revenue share for paid)
- **Typical cost:** $0 for free newsletters, 10% of paid subscription revenue

## AI / Batch Processing

### Anthropic Claude API
- **Model:** Token-based (input + output)
- **Pricing (Claude Sonnet 4):**
  - Input: $3 per 1M tokens
  - Output: $15 per 1M tokens
- **Pricing (Claude Opus 4):**
  - Input: $15 per 1M tokens
  - Output: $75 per 1M tokens
- **Batch mode:** 50% discount on all models
- **Use case:** Batch content creation, repurposing, engagement response drafting
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** token (input + output)
- **Typical cost per social post:** $0.01-0.05 (Sonnet), $0.05-0.25 (Opus)
- **Typical cost per newsletter:** $0.05-0.15 (Sonnet), $0.25-0.75 (Opus)

### OpenAI API
- **Model:** Token-based
- **Pricing (GPT-4o):**
  - Input: $2.50 per 1M tokens
  - Output: $10 per 1M tokens
- **SOCIAL:OS unit:** token
- **Typical cost per social post:** $0.01-0.03
