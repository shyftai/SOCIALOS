# SOCIAL:OS Intake Interview

Structured onboarding interview for social media. Ask one block at a time. Confirm before moving to the next block. For any field the user skips, use defaults from defaults.md.

## Block 0 -- Role Selection

Ask: "What's your role?"
- **Social Media Manager** -- skip nothing, deep-dive on engagement and calendar
- **Content Creator** -- deep-dive on pillars, voice, and formats; light on engagement rules
- **Community Manager** -- deep-dive on engagement, community, and moderation; light on newsletter
- **Agency** -- ask about multi-workspace needs, client reporting, and clone preferences
- **Founder** -- suggest quick start; if full onboarding, keep blocks short

Save to: `workspace.config.md`

## Block 1 -- Brand Identity

- What's your brand name?
- One sentence describing what you do
- What's your brand's mission or purpose?
- What makes you different from alternatives?
- Brand values (3-5 core values)
- Any brand guidelines or style guides to reference?

Save to: `BRAND.md`

## Block 2 -- Audience

- Who is your primary audience? (demographics, psychographics)
- Where do they spend time online? (platforms, communities, subreddits)
- What are their top 3 pain points?
- What content do they engage with most? (format, topics, tone)
- What do they care about beyond your product/service?
- Secondary audiences? (investors, partners, talent)

Save to: `AUDIENCE.md`

## Block 3 -- Content Pillars

- What are your 3-5 content pillars? (main topics you'll consistently post about)
  - If unsure, suggest based on brand and audience:
    - Educational (teach your expertise)
    - Behind-the-scenes (build trust and connection)
    - Social proof (case studies, testimonials, wins)
    - Industry insights (trends, commentary, predictions)
    - Culture/values (what you stand for)
    - Product/service (features, use cases, launches)
    - Community (engagement, questions, conversations)
- For each pillar: target percentage of total content (should sum to 100%)
- Any content series or recurring formats? (e.g., "Monday Motivation", "Friday Tips")

Save to: `PILLARS.md`

## Block 4 -- Tone of Voice

- How should your social content sound? (pick 3-5 adjectives)
  - Examples: conversational, authoritative, witty, warm, bold, educational, provocative
- Any words or phrases to always use?
- Any words or phrases to never use?
- How do you handle humor? (often, occasionally, never, only on specific platforms)
- Paste an example post you love (yours or someone else's)
- Platform-specific tone adjustments:
  - LinkedIn: more professional?
  - Twitter: more casual/witty?
  - Instagram: more visual/lifestyle?
  - TikTok: more raw/unfiltered?

Save to: `TOV.md`

## Block 5 -- Channels and Platforms

- Which platforms are you active on? (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, others)
- For each active platform:
  - Current follower count
  - Current posting frequency
  - Target posting frequency
  - Primary goal (awareness, engagement, leads, community)
  - Any platform-specific constraints?
- Which platform is your priority? (where you'll invest most effort)

Save to: `CHANNELS.md`

## Block 6 -- Posting Strategy

- What days and times do you want to post? (per platform)
- Minimum posts per week per platform?
- Maximum posts per day per platform?
- Any blackout periods? (weekends, holidays, company events)
- Content batching preference? (daily, weekly, bi-weekly)

Save to: `CALENDAR.md` (template) and `CHANNELS.md`

## Block 7 -- Engagement Rules

- Daily engagement time budget? (e.g., 30 min/day)
- Engagement targets:
  - Comments to respond to per day
  - Proactive engagements per day (commenting on others' posts)
  - DM response time target
- Target accounts to engage with regularly (thought leaders, partners, prospects)
- Community platforms to participate in (Reddit, Discord, Slack groups, forums)
- Engagement voice: same as posts or different? (e.g., more casual in comments)

Save to: `ENGAGEMENT.md`

## Block 8 -- Newsletter Setup

- Do you have a newsletter? (yes/no/planning to start)
- If yes:
  - Platform (Beehiiv, ConvertKit, Substack, Mailchimp, other)
  - Current subscriber count
  - Frequency (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
  - Format (curated, original, update, hybrid)
  - Current open rate and click rate (if known)
- If planning to start:
  - Preferred platform?
  - Target frequency?
  - Content type?

Save to: `NEWSLETTER.md`

## Block 9 -- Tools and Integrations

- Scheduling tool? (Buffer, Typefully, Later, Hootsuite, native scheduling)
- Analytics tools? (platform native, Sprout Social, Buffer Analytics)
- Design tools? (Canva, Figma, Adobe)
- Video tools? (CapCut, Descript, Canva)
- Newsletter platform? (covered in Block 8)
- CRM or link tracking? (UTM parameters, Bitly)
- Any other tools in your stack?

Save to: `TOOLS.md`

## Block 10 -- Competitors

- Top 3-5 competitors or accounts you admire on social?
- For each: which platform(s) and their handle
- What do they do well?
- What do they miss or do poorly?
- How do you want to differentiate?

Save to: `COMPETITORS.md`

## Block 11 -- Goals and Roadmap

- Follower growth targets per platform (30/60/90 day)
- Engagement rate targets
- Content volume targets
- What does success look like in 90 days?
- Any upcoming launches, events, or campaigns to plan for?
- Long-term vision for social presence (6-12 months)

Save to: `ROADMAP.md`

---

After all blocks: display summary of populated files and suggest next steps based on role.
