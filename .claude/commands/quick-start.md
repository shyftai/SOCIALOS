# SOCIAL:OS Quick Start

5-block quick onboarding. Get running in minutes. Remaining fields use defaults from defaults.md.

## Block 1 -- Brand
"What's your brand? One sentence about what you do."
Example: "We help B2B SaaS companies grow through content-led sales"

Save to: `workspace.config.md` and `BRAND.md`

## Block 2 -- Audience
"Who are you creating content for? Give me a person and their role."
Example: "Startup founders building their first GTM team"

Save to: `AUDIENCE.md`

## Block 3 -- Platforms
"Which platforms are you active on? Pick your top 1-3."
Options: LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Newsletter
Default: LinkedIn

Save to: `CHANNELS.md`

## Block 4 -- Voice
"How should your content sound? Pick 2-3 adjectives."
Examples: conversational and bold, professional and warm, witty and educational
Default: conversational and authoritative

Save to: `TOV.md`

## Block 5 -- First Pillar
"What's the #1 topic you want to be known for?"
Example: "Practical GTM strategy for early-stage startups"

Save to: `PILLARS.md` (additional pillars suggested based on brand and audience)

---

## After quick start:

1. Pre-fill all remaining workspace files with defaults from defaults.md:
   - ENGAGEMENT.md, CALENDAR.md, NEWSLETTER.md, TOOLS.md
   - COMPETITORS.md, HASHTAGS.md, COMPLIANCE.md
   - LEARNINGS.md, ROADMAP.md

2. Auto-generate 2-3 additional content pillars based on the brand and audience provided

3. Display: "Quick start complete. Here's what I set up:"
   - Show populated files with key values
   - Show files using defaults (can be refined later)

4. Suggest next step based on role:
   - Social Media Manager: `/social:calendar` -- set up your content calendar
   - Content Creator: `/social:write` -- create your first post
   - Community Manager: `/social:engage` -- set up engagement routine
   - Founder: `/social:brief` -- create your first content brief
   - Agency: `/social:onboard {next-client}` -- onboard your next client
