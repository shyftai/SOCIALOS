# Tone of Voice Guide

## Core Voice Attributes

Define 3-5 attributes that describe how the brand sounds everywhere.

### 1. [Attribute — e.g., "Direct"]

- **Means:** We say what we mean without filler.
- **Do:** Get to the point. Use short sentences. Lead with the insight.
- **Don't:** Ramble. Hedge. Use passive voice.

### 2. [Attribute — e.g., "Knowledgeable"]

- **Means:** We earn trust by showing expertise without being condescending.
- **Do:** Cite specifics. Use data. Explain clearly.
- **Don't:** Use jargon for the sake of it. Talk down. Overcomplicate.

### 3. [Attribute — e.g., "Approachable"]

- **Means:** We sound like a smart friend, not a textbook.
- **Do:** Use conversational language. Address the reader directly.
- **Don't:** Be overly formal. Use corporate-speak. Sound robotic.

## Platform Adjustments

### LinkedIn

- **Tone shift:** More professional, insight-led, thought-leadership oriented
- **Sentence length:** Medium to long
- **Emojis:** Sparingly, if at all
- **Hashtags:** 3-5, industry-relevant
- **POV:** First person (personal brand) or "we" (company)

### Twitter/X

- **Tone shift:** More casual, punchy, opinionated
- **Sentence length:** Short. Fragment-friendly.
- **Emojis:** Moderate, used for emphasis
- **Hashtags:** 0-2
- **POV:** First person, conversational

### Instagram

- **Tone shift:** More visual/emotional, storytelling-driven
- **Sentence length:** Varies by format (caption vs. carousel text)
- **Emojis:** Yes, as visual breaks
- **Hashtags:** 5-15 in first comment or caption
- **POV:** First person, relatable

### TikTok

- **Tone shift:** Most casual, trend-aware, fast-paced
- **Sentence length:** Very short
- **Emojis:** As needed
- **Hashtags:** 3-5, mix trending + niche
- **POV:** First person, unfiltered

### YouTube

- **Tone shift:** Educational, longer-form, narrative
- **Sentence length:** Natural spoken cadence
- **CTAs:** Clear, specific, once per video
- **POV:** First person

### Threads

- **Tone shift:** Conversational, community-oriented
- **Sentence length:** Short to medium
- **Hashtags:** Minimal
- **POV:** First person

### Reddit

- **Tone shift:** Authentic, value-first, no selling
- **Sentence length:** Medium, clear
- **Self-promotion:** Only when genuinely relevant
- **POV:** First person, peer-to-peer

## Community Engagement Voice

- **Replies:** Warm, helpful, never defensive
- **DMs:** Professional but personal
- **Comments on others' posts:** Add value, don't just agree
- **Handling criticism:** Acknowledge, clarify, redirect
