## Daily Engagement Routine

Complete this checklist every day.

### Morning (15 min)

- [ ] Check and respond to all DMs
- [ ] Reply to comments on recent posts
- [ ] Check mentions and tags

### Midday (15 min)

- [ ] Engage with 5-10 posts from target accounts
- [ ] Comment on 3-5 posts in your niche
- [ ] Share or repost 1 relevant piece of content

### Evening (10 min)

- [ ] Reply to any new comments
- [ ] Check DMs again
- [ ] Note any engagement patterns for LEARNINGS.md

## Engagement Priorities

Who to engage with first, in order:

1. **Customers / users** -- Always respond first
2. **Prospects / leads** -- High value, warm them up
3. **Industry peers** -- Build relationships
4. **Influencers in niche** -- Get on their radar
5. **General audience** -- Maintain community feel

---

## Escalation Rules

| Situation              | Action                               |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Negative comment       | Acknowledge, respond calmly, take to DM if needed |
| Brand mention (positive) | Like, reply with thanks, reshare if appropriate |
| Brand mention (negative) | Assess validity, respond if warranted, flag internally |
| Spam / trolls          | Ignore or block, do not engage        |
| Press / media inquiry  | Do not respond publicly, escalate to [owner] |
| Crisis / PR issue      | Pause all scheduled posts, escalate immediately |
