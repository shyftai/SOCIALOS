# Acceptable Use Policy

SOCIAL:OS is designed for legitimate social media management, content creation, and audience engagement. By using this software, you agree to the following:

## Do
- Comply with all platform terms of service (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, Threads, and any other connected platforms)
- Respect copyright and intellectual property -- only use content you have rights to
- Disclose sponsored, paid, or gifted content as required by FTC, ASA, and equivalent local regulations
- Maintain and follow brand guidelines for every account you manage
- Use only legitimately sourced content, images, and media (properly licensed or original)
- Honour platform rate limits and API usage policies
- Credit original creators when sharing or repurposing their content
- Comply with all applicable privacy, data protection, and advertising laws
- Include required disclosures in newsletter emails (physical address, unsubscribe link)
- Process unsubscribe and data deletion requests within required timeframes

## Do Not
- Buy fake followers, likes, comments, shares, or any form of artificial engagement
- Violate platform terms of service through automation, scraping, or circumvention of restrictions
- Post misleading, deceptive, or manipulated content presented as authentic
- Harass, spam, or engage in coordinated inauthentic behaviour
- Use bots for fake engagement, astroturfing, or manufactured social proof
- Scrape private or personal data from social platforms
- Impersonate individuals, brands, or organizations
- Post content that incites violence, discrimination, or hatred
- Add subscribers to newsletters without explicit consent
- Process sensitive personal data (health, financial, political, biometric) without appropriate safeguards and consent
- Fabricate metrics, testimonials, or proof points in any content

## Platform Automation Warning
Several platforms explicitly restrict or prohibit third-party automation:

- **LinkedIn** prohibits automation tools. Use of SOCIAL:OS with LinkedIn automation tools (including Crispy) is at your own risk and may result in account restrictions or permanent bans.
- **Instagram** restricts automated interactions. Automated liking, following, commenting, or messaging may result in action blocks or account suspension.
- **Twitter/X** rate-limits API access and prohibits certain automated behaviours. Ensure your usage complies with their current automation rules.
- **TikTok** restricts automated interactions and content posting through unofficial means.

Shyft AI is not responsible for any actions taken by any platform against your account.

## Enforcement
This policy is advisory. SOCIAL:OS is open-source software -- Shyft AI cannot monitor or control how it is used. Users bear full responsibility for their compliance with applicable laws and platform policies.
